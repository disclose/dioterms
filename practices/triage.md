# Triage

> **Status:** Placeholder. This page is part of the framework skeleton. Community contributions fill in the substance over time — [open a PR](https://github.com/disclose/dioterms).

The daily operating reality of a VDP: reports come in, and someone has to sort them. Triage is where a VDP lives or dies — fast, consistent, fair triage turns researchers into allies; slow or opaque triage turns them into critics.

- **Routing** — handoff to product teams, SLA tracking, ownership
- **Communication cadence during triage** — what to tell researchers and when
- **When to pay** (for BBPs) — decision frameworks for payout timing

## How to contribute

Open a PR against `practices/triage.md` with specific guidance from your experience. This is high-leverage territory — a good triage section helps every new program avoid predictable mistakes.
