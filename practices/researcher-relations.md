# Researcher Relations

> **Status:** Placeholder. This page is part of the framework skeleton. Community contributions fill in the substance over time — [open a PR](https://github.com/disclose/dioterms).

Your VDP lives or dies on the reputation of how you treat the researchers who engage with it. Good researcher relations is cheap, visible, and compounds over years. Bad researcher relations is expensive, public, and hard to recover from.

## Planned sections

- **Communication cadence** — acknowledgement windows, status updates, close-out notifications
- **Hall of fame / recognition** — what to include, what to omit, attribution preferences
- **Rewards and swag** (non-BBP) — non-cash recognition options
- **Handling disagreements** — severity disputes, scope disputes, payout disputes
- **Escalation and appeals** — internal paths, ombudsperson models, external mediation
- **Researcher reputation signals** — how to weight track record in triage
- **When to sever the relationship** — abuse, extortion, repeated bad-faith engagement
- **The public face** — blog posts, conference talks, program transparency reports

## How to contribute

Open a PR against `practices/researcher-relations.md`. Researchers AND program owners both welcome — the best guidance comes from both sides.
