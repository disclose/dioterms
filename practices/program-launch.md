# Program Launch

> **Status:** Placeholder. This page is part of the framework skeleton. Community contributions fill in the substance over time — [open a PR](https://github.com/disclose/dioterms).

Everything between "we're thinking about a VDP" and "we've gone live." The preflight decisions that determine whether your program will be a net positive or a net fire.

## Planned sections

- **Readiness check** — signals you're ready to accept reports (security.txt, intake channel, an owner, a triage path)
- **VDP vs. BBP** — when to pick which, and what it commits you to
- **Scoping** — what to include, what to explicitly exclude, and how to communicate drift
- **Stakeholder alignment** — Legal, PR, Engineering, Security, Executive — who signs off on what
- **Terms selection** — choosing the right `terms/` boilerplate as your starting point
- **Soft launch vs. hard launch** — invite-only ramps, staged rollouts, platform choice
- **Go-live checklist** — the last-mile sanity checks before your policy becomes externally reachable

## How to contribute

This page is a stub. If you've run a VDP launch and have hard-won guidance to share, open a PR against `practices/program-launch.md`. Short, specific, opinionated is better than long and hedged.
